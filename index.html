ALBANY, NEW YORK is full of world-class attractions and hidden gems.

THE EGG PERFORMING ARTS CENTER features unforgettable modernist architecture, acoustic excellence, and hosts world-class concerts and theatre productions year-round.

WASHINGTON PARK offers a serene escape from the city hustle. Stroll along its pathways, admire the tulips in spring, or simply relax by the lake.

THE PALACE THEATER serves as a premier performing arts venue, hosting a diverse array of events including concerts, Broadway productions, comedy shows, dance performances, and much more.

CHERRY HILL PARK is a charming historic site located in the South End neighborhood. The gardens feature a variety of flowers, shrubs, and trees, creating a tranquil atmosphere. Benches scattered throughout the grounds invite visitors to sit and enjoy the scenery.

STATE UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK (SUNY) AT ALBANY hosts public lectures, workshops, and conferences on various topics, allowing visitors to engage with cutting-edge research and ideas.

Whether you're interested in history, nature, arts, or cuisine, Albany has something to offerevery visitor.
