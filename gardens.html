Washington Park

Located in the heart of Albany, NY, Washington Park is the city's main park and a Capital Region hotspot for outdoor activities, events, entertainment, and more. Spanning approximately 89 acres, it's one of the most beautifully sculpted and well-maintained parks around, making it a great place to visit during your time in Albany. The park is open Sunrise-11pm and is open to the public.

State and Willet Streets Albany, NY 12210 (518) 434-5699 www.washingtonparkconservancy.org

Pine Hollow Arboretum

The Pine Hollow Arboretum is a 25-acre, member-supported, not-for-profit arboretum and nature preserve situated five miles from downtown Albany, in Slingerlands, New York. The arboretum's cataloged collection consists of over 3,300 unique trees, shrubs and other woody plants from around the world. Admission to the arboretum is FREE and open to the public 365 days a year, from dawn until dusk.

34 Pine Hollow Rd Slingerlands, NY 12159 (518) 992-2033 pinehollowarboretum.org

Ten Broeck Mansion

Ten Broeck Mansion is located in Albany's Arbor Hill neighborhood. Cared for by the Cornell Cooperative Extension's Master Gardener Volunteer Program, the luscious gardens flourish throughout the spring and summer months. With flowers ranging from tulips, daffodils, forget-me-nots, irises, lilacs, lily-of-the-valleys, its sure to be an enjoyable experience. Admission is FREE ,and open to the public from dawn until dusk.

9 Ten Broeck Place Albany, NY 12210 518-436-9826 tenbroeckmansion.org

Pruyn House

Formily the country estate of Casparus Francis Pruyn, the estate is located in Colonie. Featuring a herb and flower garden maintained by the Fort Orange Garden Club. Walk the paths and enjoy the scenery. Connect with nature in this peaceful garden. Admission is FREE. Open to the public Monday to Friday from 9am to 3pm.

207 Old Niskayuna Rd Latham, NY 12110 Phone: (518) 783-1435 www.pruynhouse.org

Historic Cherry Hill

Historic Cherry Hill is located on South Pearl Street in Albany. It was originally owned by the Van Rensselaer family. Some flower beds dating back to the 1930s you can explore the garden for free. Gardens are open Fridays from 1pm to 4pm, and Saturdays from 10am to 4pm.

523½ South Pearl Street Albany, NY 12202 518-434-4791 historiccherryhill.org
