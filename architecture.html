The Empire State Plaza in Albany is around 98 acres of many buildings of art accompanied by actual sculptures of art. Nelson A. Rockefeller was embarrassed and inspired when the Princess of the Netherlands came to visit. He thought it was not what the Princess had expected, in turn he made it a personal mission of his to bring about admirable change. The Empire State Plaza would be the result. There are many buildings in the city of Albany that are wonderfully designed however, the Empire Plaza also has an underground city the Concourse, that connects all of the State buildings. An architectural marvel. In the year 1911 the Capitol Building was in a blaze destroying the New York State Museum and the New York State Library, ironically. Over 800,000 documents and books. A true loss, nevertheless we have an account or record (that the fire actually occurred). The cause is to this day unknown, blaming it on a cigar and or faulty wiring with only one casualty- the watchman or the only notable witness.
